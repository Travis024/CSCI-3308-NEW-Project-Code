LivBoulder

About

LivBoulder connects people to outdoor adventures based off of their preferences. Simply create a profile and take our activity quiz to explore all of the opportunity Boulder has to offer in the great outdoors. Wheter you are an advanced hiker, or just want to find an easy walking trail, LivBoulder has you covered.

Mission Statement

LivBoulder strives to inspire people to live bolder, more exciting, and more fulfilling lives by connecting them with outdoor experiences right in their own backyard.
